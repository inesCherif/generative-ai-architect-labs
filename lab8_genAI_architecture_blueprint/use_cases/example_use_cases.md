## Use Case 1: Legal Contract Analyzer

### Problem Statement

Law firms spend 15-20 hours per week manually reviewing contracts for risk clauses. This is:

- Time-consuming and expensive
- Prone to human error
- Difficult to scale
- Creates bottlenecks in deal closings

### Target Users

- Contract lawyers
- Legal operations teams
- In-house counsel
- Compliance officers

### Current Solution & Limitations

**Current:** Manual review using Word + highlighters

- Inconsistent coverage (missed clauses)
- No standardization across reviewers
- Cannot analyze large contract volumes
- No audit trail

### Proposed GenAI Solution

**AI-Powered Contract Analyzer** that:

1. Ingests contracts (PDF, DOCX)
2. Identifies key clauses (indemnity, termination, liability)
3. Flags risks based on company policies
4. Generates executive summary
5. Suggests redlines

### Data Needed

- Historical contracts (1000+)
- Company risk policies
- Clause libraries
- Legal ontology (contract relationships)
- Industry standards

### Success Metrics

- **Time savings:** 80% reduction in review time
- **Accuracy:** 95% clause detection rate
- **Coverage:** 100% clause scanning (vs 70-80% manual)
- **Cost:** ROI positive in 6 months

## Use Case 2: Healthcare Research Copilot

### Problem Statement

Biotech researchers spend hours searching through scientific literature to:

- Find relevant studies
- Understand drug mechanisms
- Identify clinical trial results
- Track research trends

Current tools (PubMed, Google Scholar) return too many irrelevant results.

### Target Users

- Biotech researchers
- Pharmaceutical scientists
- Medical researchers
- Clinical trial coordinators

### Current Solution & Limitations

**Current:** Keyword search in PubMed + manual reading

- Information overload (thousands of papers)
- Miss connections between studies
- Can't track drug-disease relationships
- No synthesis across papers

### Proposed GenAI Solution

**AI Research Copilot** that:

1. Searches across multiple databases (PubMed, clinicaltrials.gov)
2. Understands drug-disease-mechanism relationships
3. Synthesizes findings across papers
4. Identifies research gaps
5. Generates literature summaries

### Data Needed

- PubMed abstracts
- Clinical trial data
- Drug databases (DrugBank)
- Disease ontologies (SNOMED, ICD)
- Research papers (full text)

### Success Metrics

- **Time savings:** 60% reduction in literature review time
- **Comprehensiveness:** Find 40% more relevant papers
- **Quality:** 90% user satisfaction
- **Impact:** Accelerate research by 3 months per project

## Use Case 3: Customer Support AI Assistant

### Problem Statement

E-commerce company receives 10,000 support tickets per week:

- 70% are repetitive questions
- Average response time: 24 hours
- Customer satisfaction: 65%
- Support costs: $500K/year

### Target Users

- Customers (self-service)
- Support agents (AI assistant)
- Support managers (analytics)

### Current Solution & Limitations

**Current:** Zendesk + human agents

- Long wait times
- Inconsistent answers
- Agents spend time on simple questions
- No 24/7 coverage

### Proposed GenAI Solution

**AI Support Assistant** that:

1. Answers common questions automatically (returns, shipping, account)
2. Escalates complex issues to humans
3. Provides agents with suggested responses
4. Learns from previous tickets
5. Available 24/7

### Data Needed

- Historical support tickets (50K+)
- Product documentation
- FAQ database
- Order/customer data
- Company policies

### Success Metrics

- **Automation rate:** 70% tickets fully automated
- **Response time:** <1 minute for automated, <4 hours for escalated
- **Satisfaction:** Increase from 65% to 85%
- **Cost savings:** $350K/year (70% reduction)

## Use Case 4: Internal Knowledge Search Engine

### Problem Statement

Large company (5000+ employees) has knowledge scattered across:

- Confluence wikis
- Google Docs
- Slack conversations
- Email threads
- SharePoint

Employees spend 3-5 hours/week searching for information.

### Target Users

- All employees
- New hires (onboarding)
- Product teams
- Engineering teams

### Current Solution & Limitations

**Current:** Search each platform individually

- Have to know where to look
- Results are keyword-based (miss semantic matches)
- No cross-platform search
- Stale/outdated content

### Proposed GenAI Solution

**Unified AI Search Engine** that:

1. Searches across all platforms
2. Understands natural language questions
3. Provides synthesized answers with sources
4. Identifies subject matter experts
5. Suggests related content

### Data Needed

- Confluence pages
- Google Drive documents
- Slack messages (public channels)
- Wiki pages
- Product documentation

### Success Metrics

- **Time savings:** 80% reduction in search time (3.5 hours → 40 min/week)
- **Coverage:** Search all platforms from one place
- **Accuracy:** 90% find what they need in first try
- **Productivity:** Save 3M employee hours/year

- Risk management teams
- Auditors

- Cannot understand context

### Proposed GenAI Solution

**AI Compliance Monitor** that:

1. Analyzes transactions in real-time
2. Reviews communications (email, chat)
3. Flags policy violations with explanations
4. Generates compliance reports
5. Learns from compliance officer feedback

### Data Needed

- Historical transactions
- Compliance policies and regulations

- Risk taxonomies

### Success Metrics

- **Detection rate:** 95% of violations caught (vs 80%)
- **False positives:** Reduce from 80% to 20%
- **Speed:** Real-time vs 24-hour delay
- **Risk reduction:** Avoid $2M in potential fines

## How to Choose Your Use Case

### Questions to Ask:

1. **Do I understand the domain?**
   - Choose something you're familiar with or interested in

2. **Is there a clear problem?**
   - Quantify: time wasted, money lost, errors made

3. **Can AI actually help?**
   - Problem involves: search, summarization, analysis, generation

4. **Is data available?**
   - Need existing data or ability to create it

5. **Can I measure success?**
   - Clear metrics for improvement
